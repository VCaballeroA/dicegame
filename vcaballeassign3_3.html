How to Play

For "Highest Number Wins"
The objective of the game is to get the highest (biggest) number possible. Click on one of the four coloured boxes to roll your virtual dice and land on a number between 1 to 6. The player with the highest four digit number wins the game!

For "Lowest Number Wins"
The objective of the game is to get the lowest (smallest) number possible. Click on one of the four coloured boxes to roll your virtual dice and land on a number between 1 to 6. The player with the lowest four digit number wins the game!
